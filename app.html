Inital position

  • Dancers form a square

1st movement

  • beat 1-6 Dancers take each other by hand and form a circle. Starting witf right feet with 6 "polka"s dancers move the circle counterclockwise
  • beat 7-8Dancers, standing towards center of the circle release the hands and clap their hands 3 times
  • beat 9-16Dancers repeat same actions from beat 1-8, but moving the circle clockwise. At the beat 16 form a square

2nd movement

  • beat 1-8First half of pairs with waist-shoulder grasp dance the round polka towards center. When danced halfcircle around each other they return in their initial places
  • beat 9-16Dancers repeat same actions from beat 1-8, but moving the circle clockwise. At the beat 16 form a square

3rd movement

  • beat 1-6Pairs at the corners join their right hands and form little windmills. With 6 polkas they move clockwise. Girls have their free gands placed in their waist, boys rise their hands in the air.
  • beat 7-8Dancers clap their hands 3 times
  • beat 9-16Dancers repeat same actions from beat 1-8, but joining their left hands moving the circle counterclockwise.
